About This App

RoboRadio was created by Mike Riccardi


RoboRadio (working title) was created with chronic music-shufflers in mind. Our mission is to focus on quality rather than quantity to deliver the best music streams and variety that the world has to offer. This project is open source MIT licensed and available to branch on Github. We welcome your feedback! Please report any bugs or issues using the contact link.
Known issues: Sometimes a music-stream may be unavailable [if it is full or offline] When this happens you will see the speaker animation, but there will be no sound. This is normal and we have no control over it because we rely on external sources for our streams. If this happens often on a particular station please let us know so we can consider replacing the station.
Requests: If you have a favorite MP3 stream to add to our app send us a link!
Enjoy!
